ViMove

ViMove is a health application developed by two high school students that integrates artificial intelligence and image processing to make the treatment processes for the physiological ailments of elderly individuals much more accessible and practical. Users enter different parameters such as age, gender, and illness, and the application creates appropriate exercise sets for their ailments, tracking the execution of the exercises through the application. The current demo version focuses on Parkinson's disease.
